# BTC 15m MA25/50 Fixed-Notional Crossover Backtest

## Strategy Details

- Dataset: `btc/data/BTCUSD_data_15m.csv`
- Tested UTC date range: `2021-05-11` through `2026-05-10`
- Signal source: BTC-USD 15m close
- MA rule: 25-SMA and 50-SMA of 15m closes computed fresh from the BTC dataset
- Entry rule: go long when 25-SMA crosses from below/equal to above 50-SMA.
- Reversal rule: go short when 25-SMA crosses from above/equal to below 50-SMA, and reverse the live position at the same next-candle boundary.
- Execution rule: signals are evaluated on completed candles; entries and reversals are booked at the next candle boundary using the signal candle close.
- Gap rule: force exit before gap and wait for next completed post gap candle.
- Accounting: fixed `$100000.00` notional per trade, no costs, no compounding position sizing, and points/USD metrics reported together.
